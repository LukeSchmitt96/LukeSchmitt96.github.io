Pendulum Simulation

This page shows a basic simulation of a simple pendulum.

We will first derive the dynamics of the pendulum.

Calculate the force acting on the pendulum

Derive the equations of motion using Lagrangian mechanics.

$$ ml^2\ddot{\theta(t)}+mgl\cos(\theta(t)) = -b\dot{\theta}(t) + u(t) $$

\begin{align} \dot{x} & = f(x,u) + g(x)*u \\ & = (u - mgl\cos(\theta))/b \\ \end{align}
